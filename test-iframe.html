Testing Instructions:

  2. Switch to payment iframe: browser-cli frame switch --selector "#payment-frame"
  3. Fill iframe input: browser-cli fill "#card-number" "4242-4242-4242-4242"
  4. Switch back to main: browser-cli frame --main
  5. Fill main input: browser-cli fill "#main-input" "Main frame text"
